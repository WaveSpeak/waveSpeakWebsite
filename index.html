Our story and vision

We believe in the power of personalization. That's why our vision is to build an affordable tablet that truly allows you to make it your own.

Core-Principals

Open-source

OpenSpeak is the perfect solution to help individuals with communication difficulties. Our AAC tablet is built using Java and the open-source software allows for complete customization. The user has complete control over how they communicate, making this device a life-changer for many. Our team is committed to providing the most effective communication tools for individuals with special needs.

Cost-Effective

At WaveSpeak, we are committed to providing cost-effective solutions for our clients without sacrificing quality. Our 3D printed technology and use of simple components allows us to keep costs down while still delivering a durable product.

What is AAC?

AAC is used by people who, some or all of the time, cannot rely on their speech. AAC incorporates the individual's full communication abilities and may include any existing speech or vocalizations, gestures, manual signs, and aided communication. AAC is truly multimodal...
